P*ssibilities

Design workshop exploring new possibilities through waste with Andreu Carulla

What to expect

Andreu Carulla is a Catalan-born experimental designer whose award-winning works focus around materiality while embodying craftsmanship and sustainability. As a part of the N*thing Is Possible exhibition by Potato Head, OMA & Friends, workshops will be conducted by Carulla, who will be presenting his latest innovation and process, the Styro-wrap. This technique manipulates and transforms discarded styrofoam into a beautiful and highly functional material. For the purpose of this workshop, you will learn to apply this technique to kitchen utensils.
